Agenda

8:15 -  9:00 a.m.  Registration

Complimentary breakfast and coffee

9:00 - 10:30 a.m. Session 1: Pathologies of Business

Ed Balleisen, Fraud: An American History from Barnum to Madoff
Eugene Soltes, Why They Do It: Inside the Mind of the White Collar Criminal

Commentators: Susanna Blumenthal, Leo Katz, Joe McGrath, Jed Rakoff

June Carbone, Shafted: How Women Lose in a Winner Take All Economy 

Commentator: Kathy Baker

11:30 - 12:15 p.m. Session 3: Even More Pathologies of Business

Gerrit De Geest, Rents: How Marketing Causes Inequality

Commentator: Claire Hill

12:15 - 12:30 p.m. Break

David Enrich, Dark Towers

Commentator: Eugene Soltes

1:30 - 1:45 p.m. Break

Andrew Lo, Adaptive Markets

Commentators: Dan Awrey, Brett McDonnell

2:45 - 3:00 p.m. Break

3:00 - 3:45 p.m.   Session 5: Innovation

Tom Cotter, Patent Wars

Commentator: Andrew Torrance

Using the Salz Review, on Barclays’ banking practices as a point of departure, this panel will discuss bad culture and good culture, and how to move from one to the other.

Commentators: John Borrows, William Casebeer, Thomas Talhelm

Additional Participant: Travis Laster
