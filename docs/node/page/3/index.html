Agenda

8:15 -  9:00 a.m.  Registration

Complimentary breakfast and coffee

9:00 - 10:30 a.m. Session 1: Pathologies of Business

Ed Balleisen, Fraud: An American History from Barnum to Madoff
Eugene Soltes, Why They Do It: Inside the Mind of the White Collar Criminal

Commentators: Susanna Blumenthal, Leo Katz, Joe McGrath, Jed Rakoff

Speakers

Join conference speakers from Pennsylvania, New York, Illinois, Delaware, Michigan, Kansas, Missouri, Massachusetts, Canada, Ireland, and Minnesota for an all-day discussion about business and the books they've written on the subject.

 

Dan Awrey

Biography

CANCELED: Law & Economics Conference 2020

Given the situation involving COVID-19 (coronavirus), we have decided to CANCEL the Books on Business Conference. We intend to reschedule when we can safely do so. If you have any questions, please contact lawevent@umn.edu. 

At this conference, we will discuss several new books on business. Each panel will feature one or more books; the authors will make remarks, as will commentators. The concluding panel, on culture, will feature three scholars discussing how their work could inform analyses of culture in financial institutions.
